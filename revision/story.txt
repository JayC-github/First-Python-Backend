# user story for businesses users

- As a businesses user, I want to donate my item to those who may need them so that I can get rid of excess items that I no longer need.

- As a businesses user, I want to have information about people who may looking for my item so that I can find a potential reciever.

- As a businesses user, I want to make a post as doner so that I can donate my item I no longer need.

# user story for organisation/indicidual users

- As a user, I want to post my information and desription of the item I need so that I can find a potential doner.
- As a user, I want to find the contact information about doner so that I can contact with them and get what I need.
